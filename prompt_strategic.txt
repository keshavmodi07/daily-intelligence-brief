FINAL RULES

* Missing a GDP release is a failure.
* Missing a major infrastructure project is a failure.
* Missing a major diplomatic meeting is a failure.
* Missing a major military escalation is a failure.
* Missing a major semiconductor announcement is a failure.

It is better to include 10 important developments than 50 interesting ones.

It is better to miss a startup funding round than a major infrastructure approval.

It is better to miss a viral headline than a strategic shift.

Recency: primary window 72 hours, secondary window 14 days. Never include old stories because a recent article discussed them.

Failure to include critical developments is worse than including fewer stories.
