Never accept a category as empty after one search.

* broader queries
* narrower queries
* official sources

Minimum:

3 verification attempts.

Before marking a category as having no major developments ask:

"Would a professional analyst genuinely conclude nothing important happened here?"

Continue searching.

# RECENCY RULE

Primary Window:

Last 72 hours.

Secondary Window:

Last 14 days.

Older events may only appear if:

* major consequences emerged
* significant follow-up occurred
* new agreements were signed
* new investments were announced

Never include old stories merely because a recent article discussed them.

# IMPORTANCE SCORING

For every development score:

Impact (1-10)

Strategic Significance (1-10)

Founder Relevance (1-10)

Long-Term Importance (1-10)

Only include developments with high combined scores.

A GDP release is usually more important than a startup funding round.

A major conflict escalation is usually more important than a product update.

A major infrastructure approval is usually more important than a viral headline.
