# Builder Intelligence Brief

## Today's Alpha

- **SpaceXAI launches Grok 4.5, its smartest AI model for coding and agentic tasks**  
  SpaceXAI announced the launch of Grok 4.5, an advanced AI model excelling in coding, agentic workflows, and knowledge work. Trained on extensive datasets in coding, science, engineering, and math using tens of thousands of NVIDIA GB300 GPUs, Grok 4.5 outperforms leading competitors in real engineering tasks. This marks a major step in AI-assisted software development and automation (xAI News, https://x.ai/news/grok-4-5).

- **Together AI closes $800M Series C funding and secures 500 MW compute capacity**  
  Together AI raised $800 million led by Aramco Ventures, NVIDIA, and Vista Equity, alongside commitments for over 500 MW of compute to scale its open-source AI platform for fast, efficient inference in production AI applications. This funding underlines the rising demand for scalable AI infrastructure and open innovation (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c).

- **Together AI releases FlashAttention-4 GPU kernel for Transformer models**  
  Together AI launched FlashAttention-4, a GPU kernel optimized for NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs, delivering up to 2.7x speedup in Transformer attention operations through algorithmic and kernel pipelining co-design. This innovation reduces training and inference costs for large language models, enabling more complex AI architectures (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/flashattention-4).

- **Tata Electronics and ASML sign MoU to advance semiconductor fab in India**  
  Tata Electronics partnered with ASML to deploy advanced lithography tools at its upcoming 300 mm semiconductor fab in Dholera, Gujarat. The collaboration includes talent development and supply chain initiatives, reinforcing India’s semiconductor manufacturing ambitions (ASML Newsroom, https://www.asml.com/en/news/press-releases/2026/tata-electronics-and-asml-announce-strategic-partnership).

- **xAI launches Grok Build Plugin Marketplace to extend AI coding capabilities**  
  xAI introduced a plugin marketplace within Grok Build, enabling users to browse and install modular plugins that bundle AI skills, commands, and agents. This fosters an ecosystem for AI coding tool extensibility and accelerates developer productivity (xAI News, https://x.ai/news/grok-plugin-marketplace).

- **TSMC reports 6.2% revenue increase in June 2026, driven by strong demand**  
  TSMC posted NT$442.68 billion revenue for June 2026, up 6.2% from May and 67.9% year-over-year, reflecting robust demand for advanced semiconductor manufacturing services (TSMC News, https://pr.tsmc.com/english/news/3323).

- **UN chief urges Iran and US to resume negotiations amid Gulf conflict escalation**  
  Renewed strikes between Iran and the US in the Gulf have heightened war risks. The UN Secretary-General called for restraint to avoid catastrophic regional and global economic impacts, especially on energy and maritime trade (UN News, https://news.un.org/feed/view/en/story/2026/07/1167923).

- **ESA and Poland to establish new civil security and resilience centre in Warsaw**  
  ESA and Poland announced a new centre focused on civil security and resilience to enhance European capabilities against emerging threats, leveraging space technologies (ESA, https://www.esa.int/About_Us/Corporate_news/ESA_and_Poland_to_establish_a_new_civil_security_and_resilience_centre_in_Warsaw).

- **NASA study identifies passenger comfort thresholds for air taxis**  
  NASA’s Armstrong Flight Research Center used VR motion simulators to study discomfort caused by sudden movements in air taxis, informing design and operational improvements to boost adoption of urban air mobility (NASA, https://www.nasa.gov/centers-and-facilities/armstrong/nasa-study-points-to-smoother-air-taxi-rides/).

- **RBI imposes monetary penalties on multiple cooperative banks for non-compliance**  
  The Reserve Bank of India fined several cooperative banks for governance, KYC, and housing finance scheme violations, signaling stricter regulatory enforcement to protect depositors and strengthen banking sector stability (RBI, multiple press releases).
