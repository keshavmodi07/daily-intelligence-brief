## Today's Alpha

**Together AI closes $800M Series C and secures 500 MW compute capacity**  
Together AI announced a major $800 million Series C funding round led by Aramco Ventures, NVIDIA, Vista Equity, and others, alongside commitments for over 500 MW of compute capacity to scale AI infrastructure for production workloads (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c). This positions Together AI to accelerate open-source AI model adoption and AI-native cloud platform growth.

**Tata Electronics and ASML sign MoU to advance India's semiconductor ecosystem**  
Tata Electronics and ASML formalized a strategic partnership to deploy ASML's advanced lithography tools and develop local talent for Tata’s upcoming 300 mm semiconductor fab in Dholera, Gujarat, India (ASML Newsroom, https://www.asml.com/en/news/press-releases/2026/tata-electronics-and-asml-announce-strategic-partnership). This marks a significant step in India's semiconductor self-reliance and manufacturing capability expansion.

**Together AI announces FlashAttention-4 kernel co-design for NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs**  
Together AI revealed FlashAttention-4, an optimized attention kernel co-designed for NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs that improves computation-data overlap and reduces memory traffic, enhancing transformer model training and inference speed (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/flashattention-4).

**Samsung launches industry's fastest UFS 5.0 storage for on-device AI**  
Samsung Electronics unveiled a UFS 5.0 solution achieving 10.8 GB/s data transfer speed, targeting faster storage and processing for mobile devices running on-device AI workloads (Samsung Semiconductor Newsroom, https://news.samsungsemiconductor.com/global/samsung-unveils-industrys-most-powerful-ufs-5-0-solution-for-next-gen-on-device-ai-applications).

**xAI launches Grok Build 0.1 coding model in public beta**  
xAI released Grok Build 0.1, a fast coding AI model optimized for agentic coding tasks like web development and debugging, serving over 100 tokens per second at competitive pricing (xAI News, https://x.ai/news/grok-build-0-1). This enhances AI-assisted software engineering productivity.
