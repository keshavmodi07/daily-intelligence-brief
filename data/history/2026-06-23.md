- **OpenAI launches Daybreak tools to accelerate vulnerability patching at scale**

- **NVIDIA advances autonomous telecom networks with AI agents at TM Forum’s DTW Ignite 2026**  
  NVIDIA showcased AI-driven autonomous network operations integrating synthetic data and telecom-specific models, enabling proactive network management while maintaining human oversight. This signals a shift toward resilient, AI-powered telecom infrastructure (Nvidia Blog, https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/telecom-ai-agents-dtw-ignite-2026/).

- **OpenAI and Trail of Bits launch Patch the Planet to support open-source security**  
  As part of Daybreak, this initiative helps open-source maintainers identify and patch vulnerabilities with AI and expert review, addressing critical security needs in global digital infrastructure (OpenAI News, https://openai.com/index/patch-the-planet).

- **NVIDIA deploys Vera CPUs in Los Alamos National Laboratory supercomputers for scientific AI**  
  LANL’s new supercomputers Mission, Vision, and Veritas use NVIDIA Vera CPUs and Rubin GPUs to enable autonomous AI-driven scientific discovery, potentially transforming research methodologies (Nvidia Blog, https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/nvidia-vera-cpu-los-alamos-national-laboratory/).

- **Samsung Electronics deploys ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex globally**  
  Samsung rolled out OpenAI’s enterprise AI tools across divisions worldwide to boost productivity in software development, marketing, and manufacturing, marking a major corporate AI adoption (OpenAI News, https://openai.com/index/samsung-electronics-chatgpt-codex-deployment).

- **SpaceXAI and Gopuff launch Go, an AI-powered shopping assistant using Grok models**  
  Go integrates Grok multimodal AI into Gopuff’s app, personalizing shopping and generating hyper-realistic visuals, showcasing advanced consumer AI applications (xAI News, https://x.ai/news/grok-gopuff).

- **xAI introduces /goal command in Grok Build for autonomous long-running coding tasks**  
  This developer tool enables AI agents to autonomously plan, execute, and verify complex coding projects with user steering, accelerating software engineering workflows (xAI News, https://x.ai/news/introducing-goal).

- **European Parliament backs military mobility plans to facilitate transport of military assets across EU**  
  New rules include secure digital systems and infrastructure upgrades to speed military transport approvals, enhancing EU defense readiness (European Parliament, https://www.europarl.europa.eu/news/en/press-room/20260622IPR45908/).

- **NASA schedules June 30 spacewalk to repair Canadarm2 wrist joint on ISS**  
  Critical robotic arm maintenance will ensure continued ISS operational capabilities for scientific and logistical tasks (NASA, https://www.nasa.gov/news-release/nasa-to-cover-us-spacewalk-95-host-preview-news-conference/).
