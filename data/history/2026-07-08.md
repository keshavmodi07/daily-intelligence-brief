# Builder Intelligence Brief

## Today's Alpha

1. **Together AI Raises $800 Million Series C to Expand AI Compute Capacity**  
Together AI secured $800M in Series C funding led by Aramco Ventures and NVIDIA, with commitments for over 500 MW of compute capacity to scale its AI native cloud platform for inference and agent workloads. This positions Together AI to accelerate AI adoption by improving performance and cost efficiency for production AI workloads (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c).

2. **Tata Electronics and ASML Announce Strategic Partnership for Dholera Semiconductor Fab**  
Tata Electronics and ASML signed an MoU to collaborate on establishing and ramping up India’s first commercial 300 mm semiconductor fab in Dholera, Gujarat. The partnership includes deploying ASML’s advanced lithography tools and joint talent and supply chain development, strengthening India’s semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem and India-Netherlands tech collaboration (ASML Newsroom, https://www.asml.com/en/news/press-releases/2026/tata-electronics-and-asml-announce-strategic-partnership).

3. **Microsoft Announces Foundry Managed Compute with Hugging Face Models Integration**  
Microsoft unveiled Foundry Managed Compute at Build 2026, integrating Hugging Face models for one-click deployment of open-weight AI models on a managed GPU PaaS with enterprise-grade security and governance. This expands enterprise access to frontier AI models and simplifies multi-model orchestration on Azure (Hugging Face Blog, https://huggingface.co/blog/microsoft/foundry-managed-compute).

4. **Rocket Lab Completes Record Responsive Launch and On-Orbit Satellite Tracking Mission for U.S. Space Force**  
Rocket Lab successfully executed the VICTUS HAZE mission for the U.S. Space Force, completing design, build, launch, and complex on-orbit operations in record time. This demonstrates rapid threat-response capabilities and sets new standards for responsive military space operations (Rocket Lab, https://rocketlabcorp.com/updates/rocket-lab-delivers-mission-success-for-space-force-completes-historic-launch-and-on-orbit-satellite-tracking-mission-in-record-time/).

5. **NASA Seeks Industry Input on Second Phase of Commercial Space Stations**  
NASA released a draft RFP seeking feedback from American companies on the next phase of commercial space stations to ensure a smooth transition from the ISS. This fosters a competitive commercial marketplace with NASA as one customer, accelerating commercial LEO infrastructure development (NASA, https://www.nasa.gov/humans-in-space/commercial-space/nasa-seeks-industry-input-on-second-phase-of-commercial-space-stations/).

6. **Together AI Achieves ISO 27001:2022 Certification for Enterprise AI Security**  
Together AI received ISO 27001:2022 certification validating its Information Security Management System for secure, production-grade AI workloads. This enhances enterprise trust and regulatory compliance for AI deployments (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/iso-27001-2022-certification).
