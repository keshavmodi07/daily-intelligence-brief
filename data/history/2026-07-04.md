## Today's Alpha

1. **Together AI launches FlashAttention-4 kernel optimized for NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs**  
Together AI announced FlashAttention-4, a breakthrough attention kernel co-designed with hardware and algorithmic optimizations tailored for NVIDIA’s Blackwell GPU architecture. It delivers up to 2.7x speed improvements over prior versions, significantly accelerating Transformer model inference (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/flashattention-4). This advancement addresses the core bottleneck of attention computation in large AI models, enabling larger context windows and more complex AI applications.

2. **Together AI closes $800M Series C funding with major compute capacity commitments**  
Together AI secured $800 million in Series C funding from investors including Aramco Ventures, NVIDIA, and Vista Equity, alongside commitments for over 500 MW of compute capacity to scale AI infrastructure (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c). This positions the company to meet rising demand for production-grade AI workloads on an open-source AI-native cloud platform.

3. **Rocket Lab announces $8B acquisition of Iridium Communications**  
Rocket Lab signed a definitive agreement to acquire Iridium Communications, combining launch, satellite manufacturing, and a global satellite communications network into a vertically integrated space company (Rocket Lab, https://rocketlabcorp.com/updates/rocket-lab-to-acquire-iridium-in-historic-deal-creating-a-fully-vertically-integrated-space-powerhouse-primed-for-growth/). This integration enhances competitiveness in satellite communications and IoT markets.

4. **Tata Electronics and ASML sign MoU to advance India’s semiconductor fab ecosystem**  
Tata Electronics partnered with ASML to support the establishment and ramp-up of a 300 mm semiconductor fab in Dholera, Gujarat. ASML will supply advanced lithography tools and assist talent and supply chain development (ASML Newsroom, https://www.asml.com/en/news/press-releases/2026/tata-electronics-and-asml-announce-strategic-partnership). This is a strategic move to strengthen India’s semiconductor manufacturing capabilities.

5. **NASA’s Artemis II mission sets streaming viewership records**  
NASA’s live coverage of Artemis II, the first crewed lunar flyby in decades, attracted over 149 million views globally, demonstrating strong public enthusiasm for lunar exploration (NASA, https://www.nasa.gov/general/nasas-artemis-ii-breaks-agency-streaming-record/). This public engagement supports NASA’s lunar exploration agenda and potential international collaborations.
