# Builder Intelligence Brief

## Today's Alpha

- **Together AI closes $800M Series C and secures 500 MW compute capacity**  
  Together AI announced raising $800 million in Series C funding from investors including Aramco Ventures, NVIDIA, and Vista Equity, alongside commitments for over 500 MW of compute capacity to scale AI infrastructure for production-grade workloads and open-source AI platform innovation. This positions Together AI to accelerate AI adoption and developer access to large-scale compute resources (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c).

- **Together AI launches FlashAttention-4 kernel optimized for NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs**  
  Together AI released FlashAttention-4, an advanced attention kernel featuring new pipelining and hardware-software co-design to handle asymmetric hardware scaling on NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs. This improves transformer attention performance, enabling faster, more efficient large language model training and inference (Together AI Blog, https://www.together.ai/blog/flashattention-4).

- **Rocket Lab announces $8B acquisition of Iridium Communications**  
  Rocket Lab agreed to acquire Iridium Communications in a cash and stock deal, creating a vertically integrated space company combining launch, satellite manufacturing, and a global satellite communications network. This integration enables new space-based IoT and direct-to-device applications, reshaping competitive dynamics in the space sector (Rocket Lab, https://rocketlabcorp.com/updates/rocket-lab-to-acquire-iridium-in-historic-deal-creating-a-fully-vertically-integrated-space-powerhouse-primed-for-growth/).

- **Tata Electronics and ASML form strategic partnership for 300 mm semiconductor fab in Dholera, India**  
  Tata Electronics signed an MoU with ASML to collaborate on establishing and ramping up a 300 mm semiconductor fab in Gujarat, with ASML providing advanced lithography tools and support for talent and supply chain development. This is a major step toward strengthening India’s semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem and reducing import dependence (ASML Newsroom, https://www.asml.com/en/news/press-releases/2026/tata-electronics-and-asml-announce-strategic-partnership).

- **xAI launches Grok Build 0.1 coding model in public beta via API**  
  xAI released Grok Build 0.1, a fast AI coding model optimized for agentic coding tasks like web development and debugging, delivering over 100 tokens per second at competitive pricing. Available via API, this tool aims to accelerate developer productivity and enable complex autonomous coding workflows (xAI News, https://x.ai/news/grok-build-0-1).

- **DFCCIL advances funding and approvals for Dedicated Freight Corridors in India**  
  The Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited secured funding totaling Rs. 81,459 crore for Eastern and Western corridors, with additional new corridors sanctioned for DPR preparation. This infrastructure development will modernize freight transport, reduce congestion, and boost economic growth (DFCCIL, https://dfccil.com/Home/DynemicPages?MenuId=78).

- **NASA’s Hubble Space Telescope releases star cluster and nebula images for US 250th anniversary**  
  NASA shared new Hubble images of globular clusters and stellar nurseries, providing insights into stellar evolution and galactic structure while celebrating the US 250th anniversary. These images inspire public interest and scientific research in astronomy (NASA, https://science.nasa.gov/missions/hubble/nasas-hubble-spies-stellar-sparkler-for-july-4th/).

- **Reserve Bank of India announces ₹24,000 crore Treasury Bills auction on July 8, 2026**  
  RBI scheduled an auction of Government Treasury Bills across multiple tenors to manage fiscal needs and liquidity. Non-competitive bids are allowed to encourage wider participation, supporting government financing and market liquidity (RBI, https://www.rbi.org.in/scripts/BS_PressReleaseDisplay.aspx?prid=63086).
