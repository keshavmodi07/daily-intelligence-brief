## Today's Alpha

- **OpenAI and Broadcom Unveil Jalapeño, a Custom LLM-Optimized Inference Chip**  
  OpenAI and Broadcom announced Jalapeño, a custom AI inference chip tailored for large language model (LLM) workloads, including GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark. Engineering samples demonstrate significant performance per watt improvements, signaling OpenAI’s move to vertically integrate AI hardware for better efficiency and scalability (OpenAI News, https://openai.com/index/openai-broadcom-jalapeno-inference-chip).  
  *Why it matters:* This could reshape AI hardware supply chains, reduce dependency on generic GPUs, and set new performance standards.  
  *Founder opportunity:* Build AI products optimized for custom inference hardware.  
  *Investor takeaway:* Invest in AI hardware innovation and OpenAI’s vertical integration strategy.  
  *Probability:* 90% confidence.

- **TSMC Reports Record Q1 2026 Revenue and Earnings, Approves $20B Arizona Fab Expansion**  
  TSMC posted record revenue of NT$1,134.10 billion and net income of NT$572.48 billion for Q1 2026, driven by strong demand for 3nm, 5nm, and 7nm nodes. The company also debuted the A13 process technology and approved a $20 billion capital injection for its Arizona fab expansion (TSMC News, https://pr.tsmc.com/english/news/3297).  
  *Why it matters:* Reinforces TSMC’s semiconductor leadership, impacting global chip supply for AI, HPC, and mobile sectors.  
  *Founder opportunity:* Leverage TSMC’s advanced nodes for next-gen chip designs.  
  *Investor takeaway:* TSMC remains a strong semiconductor manufacturing investment.  
  *Probability:* 99% confidence.

  UNECE’s World Forum approved a global regulatory framework for fully autonomous driving systems, including safety requirements, validation, and continuous performance monitoring (UN News, https://news.un.org/feed/view/en/story/2026/06/1167797).  
  *Why it matters:* Harmonizes safety standards worldwide, accelerating autonomous vehicle deployment and consumer trust.  
  *Founder opportunity:* Build compliant autonomous vehicle technologies.  
  *Investor takeaway:* Support investments in autonomous vehicle startups and suppliers.  
  *Probability:* 95% confidence.

- **NASA Selects Rocket Lab to Launch PolSIR and TSIS-2 Earth Science Missions**  
  NASA chose Rocket Lab for three dedicated Electron rocket launches starting 2027 to deploy PolSIR CubeSats studying ice clouds and TSIS-2 solar irradiance measurement missions (NASA, https://www.nasa.gov/missions/tsis-2/nasa-selects-rocket-lab-to-launch-sun-earth-science-missions/).  
  *Why it matters:* Advances climate science and weather prediction capabilities.  
  *Founder opportunity:* Develop complementary Earth observation technologies.  
  *Investor takeaway:* Support commercial launch providers and Earth science tech.  
  *Probability:* 98% confidence.

- **Botswana Joins Artemis Accords as 68th Signatory**  
  Botswana became the sixth African nation to join the Artemis Accords, committing to peaceful and transparent space exploration (NASA, https://www.nasa.gov/organizations/oiir/nasa-welcomes-botswana-as-68th-artemis-accords-signatory/).
