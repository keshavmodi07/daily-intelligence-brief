# Builder Intelligence Brief

## Today's Alpha

- **SpaceXAI launches Grok 4.5, its smartest AI model for coding and agentic tasks**  
  SpaceXAI announced Grok 4.5, an advanced AI model excelling in coding, agentic tasks, and knowledge work. Trained on extensive datasets covering coding, science, engineering, and math, and using tens of thousands of NVIDIA GB300 GPUs, Grok 4.5 outperforms leading comparable models in real engineering tasks. This launch marks a major step in AI-assisted software development and automation workflows. [xAI News](https://x.ai/news/grok-4-5)

- **OpenAI launches GPT-Live, a new generation of voice models for natural human-AI interaction**  
  OpenAI introduced GPT-Live, enabling natural, full-duplex voice conversations with AI—allowing simultaneous listening and speaking. Powered by GPT-5.5, GPT-Live is integrated into ChatGPT Voice, enhancing the fluidity and intelligence of voice interactions. This could transform voice AI applications in consumer and enterprise domains. [OpenAI News](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-live)

- **Together AI raises $800M Series C to expand open-source AI platform**  
  Together AI secured $800 million in Series C funding led by Aramco Ventures, NVIDIA, and Vista Equity. The company offers a full-stack platform for open-source AI, focusing on fast, efficient inference for AI applications and agents. The funding includes commitments for over 500 MW of compute capacity, enabling rapid scaling of AI infrastructure. [Together AI Blog](https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c)

- **Prime Intellect raises $130M Series A to enable enterprises to build their own AI agents**  
  Prime Intellect, founded in 2024, raised $130 million led by Radical Ventures with Nvidia Ventures and Intel Capital participating. The startup provides a platform for enterprises to train and deploy custom agentic AI systems independently of frontier AI labs, including compute access and reinforcement learning frameworks. [TechCrunch Startups](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/08/prime-intellect-raises-130m-series-a-to-help-enterprises-build-their-own-ai-agents/)

- **Indian Cabinet approves Rs. 81,459 Cr funding for Eastern and Western Dedicated Freight Corridors and sanctions three new corridors**  
  The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs approved comprehensive funding for the Eastern and Western Dedicated Freight Corridors, including land costs, sourced via debt, equity, and PPP. Additionally, three new corridors (East West, East Coast, North South sub-corridor) have been approved for Detailed Project Report preparation, signaling a major boost to India’s freight infrastructure modernization. [DFCCIL](https://dfccil.com/Home/DynemicPages?MenuId=78)

- **Tata Electronics and ASML form strategic partnership to advance semiconductor manufacturing in India**  
  Tata Electronics and ASML signed an MoU to collaborate on the 300 mm semiconductor fab in Dholera, Gujarat. The partnership includes deploying ASML’s advanced lithography tools and developing domestic talent and supply chains, strengthening India’s semiconductor ecosystem. [ASML Newsroom](https://www.asml.com/en/news/press-releases/2026/tata-electronics-and-asml-announce-strategic-partnership)

- **Rocket Lab completes record-setting responsive launch and on-orbit satellite tracking mission for US Space Force**  
  Rocket Lab executed the VICTUS HAZE mission with unprecedented speed: design, build, and launch within 24 hours’ notice, commissioning within 72 hours, and complex on-orbit operations within 84 hours. This demonstrates rapid space domain awareness capabilities critical for national security. [Rocket Lab](https://rocketlabcorp.com/updates/rocket-lab-delivers-mission-success-for-space-force-completes-historic-launch-and-on-orbit-satellite-tracking-mission-in-record-time/)

- **NASA’s New Horizons spacecraft wakes from longest hibernation in good health**  
  After 321 days in hibernation, NASA’s New Horizons spacecraft is active and healthy, ready to transmit scientific data from the Kuiper Belt, advancing outer solar system exploration. [NASA](https://science.nasa.gov/missions/new-horizons/nasas-new-horizons-spacecraft-wakes-from-hibernation-in-good-health/)
